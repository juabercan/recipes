Ingredients:

    - 8 ripe tomatoes
    - 60 ml olive oil (quality)
    - 1 small onion, finely chopped
    - 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
    - 1 teaspoon of paprika
    - 1 pinch of saffron
    - 8 sliced ​​artichokes 
    - 1 eggplant, cut into small pieces
    - 100 gr of assorted mushrooms
    - 3 cups of water
    - 2 cups of short grain rice
    - 100 gr of green beans, items
    - a handful of capers, drained
    - Salt to taste

Directions:

1. Wash the tomatoes, cut the stem and base and cut them into segments. We put them in a bowl with salt and a drizzle of olive oil and mix it. We reserve

2. Heat the olive oil over medium-high heat in a 30 cm pan. Add the chopped onion, garlic, paprika, saffron and season with salt. We remove all the ingredients with a wooden spoon and let the onion soften for a few minutes.

3. Add artichokes and aubergines to the pan, cooking until tender, about 5 minutes. We add the mushrooms and continue cooking for 2 more minutes.

4. We add the rice and season it with salt. We pour enough water to cover the rice completely, about 2 and 1/2 to 3 cups, and bring it to a boil.

5. Sprinkle capers on top and green beans. Place the reserved tomatoes on top (with all the juices included).

6. We lower the temperature so that it is cooked over medium heat for 20 minutes. After that time we check if the rice is at its point, and if it lacks cooking, we add more water and cook it for a few more minutes.

7. Cover the pan to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
